## What's honestly synthetic

Called out here so the reviewer sees the disclosure before finding it:

- **Failure labels** — deterministic non-linear formula over asset features + noise. Documented in `label_source: synthetic_continuous_from_features_documented` on the training report. The reason the R² is 0.64 rather than 1.0 is because the model uses a *subset* of the label's inputs — it's learning feature interactions, not reconstructing a closed form.
- **Dependency-graph edges** — hand-seeded to give Louvain something interesting to cluster; production would ingest from real substation / feeder / interconnect data.
- **SCADA telemetry values** — realistic magnitudes and diurnal patterns, but not sampled from any real utility.
- **Work orders + inspections + field reports** — narratively plausible, generated by templated LLM prompts and seeded to look like typical CMMS records.

Everything above is disclosed either on the `GET /api/data-sources` provenance endpoint or in [docs/06_data_model.md](06_data_model.md).
